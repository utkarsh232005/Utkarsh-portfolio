A well-designed digital menu card should be stylish yet functional. Imagine a clean layout with a background color that reflects the restaurant's ambiance. For a warm and inviting feel, consider earthy tones like beige or olive green. Modern restaurants might opt for sleek black or a cool grey. Text should be clear and concise, with contrasting colors for headings and descriptions to ensure readability on a screen. A touch of style can come through high-quality food photography, or subtle design elements like borders that match the restaurant's logo or branding..
